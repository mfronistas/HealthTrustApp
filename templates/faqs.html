FAQs

Q: Where can I book an appointment for?

A: This is a Newcastle based business so only centres in and around Newcastle.

Q: What times can I book an appointment for?

A: Appointments can be booked between 09:00 and 18:00.

Q: Can I cancel an appointment?

A: Yes, click My Account at the top of the screen and click on View/Cancel Appointments from the dropdown menu. Here, you should see your current appointments and the option to cancel them by clicking the button on the right.

Q: Can I have more than one appointment booked at a time?

A: Yes, you can have multiple appointments booked for different reasons but only have one booked per day.

Q: Can I get a COVID-19 vaccine booked here?

A: We unfortunately do not currently provide this service, but can be done through the NHS Website

Q: Can I choose which doctor I will get?

A: You will be automatically assigned one of our highly qualified doctors working at your local practice.
